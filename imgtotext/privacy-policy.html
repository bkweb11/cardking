Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

ImgtoText App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app processes images locally on your device to extract text and does not transmit any data to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use, sell, or share your information with third parties. Any images selected and processed remain entirely on your device.

3. Permissions Used

  • Camera & Storage Access: To select and process images for text extraction.
  • Clipboard Access: To allow users to copy extracted text.
  • Internet Access: Only used to display ads (if applicable) and for external search functionality (e.g., Google Search for selected text).

4. Third-Party Services

If our app integrates third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob for ads or Google Cloud Vision for OCR), those services may collect data based on their privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before using such features.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously and ensure that all image processing is done locally on your device. No data is stored or shared externally.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
